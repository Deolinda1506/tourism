Discover the Beauty of Benin

Benin, located in West Africa, is a fascinating country filled with cultural and natural richness.

Location of Benin

Benin is bordered by Togo to the west, Burkina Faso and Niger to the north, Nigeria to the east, and the Gulf of Guinea to the south.

The country provides easy access to a multitude of exciting destinations in the region.
